![](title.png) *2011-2014 (Polish Conference on Computer Games Development, Poland)* I've decided to organize WGK, because back in 2010 there was no conference dedicated to game industry in Poland. There were small gatherings and game jams organized by indie developers and hobbyists, but there was no professional event for the industry itself. In August of 2010 I volunteered as a conference associate during GDC Europe. This was my way to gain knowledge on how to organize professional event on a big scale. I've then partnered with Technical University of Gdansk which provided the venue, and year later in 2011 first edition of the event took place. I was responsible for acquiring sponsors and speakers, communication with companies and event promotion. Conference was a success from the first edition, and was growing fast. I've quickly ended up managing team of 9 people and 30 volunteers. Organization of the conference ended when I moved from Poland to USA in October of 2014. [Archived website of 2013 edition.](https://web.archive.org/web/20181112093849/http://wgk2011.eti.pg.gda.pl/)
